Therapeutic Methods

EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) is an evidence-based approach for processing traumatic memories and reducing their emotional impact.

This method helps the brain reprocess disturbing experiences naturally, leading to decreased distress and more adaptive perspectives.

Trauma Treatment

ImTT

Image Transformation Therapy (ImTT) uses guided imagery to access and transform subconscious patterns contributing to emotional distress.

This gentle yet powerful approach helps clients reprocess negative self-images and develop healthier internal representations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies depending on your specific concerns and goals. Many clients experience noticeable improvement within 6-12 sessions, while complex trauma may require longer-term work. We'll discuss this during your initial consultation and regularly assess progress.

Is online therapy effective?

Research shows that online therapy can be just as effective as in-person sessions for many conditions, including anxiety and trauma. EMDR and ImTT adapt well to the online format. The most important factors are your comfort level and having a private space for sessions.

What's your cancellation policy?

I require 24 hours notice for cancellations to allow time for other clients to book the slot. Late cancellations are charged 50% of the session fee, except in cases of emergency. This policy helps maintain consistency in your treatment and respects everyone's time.

How do I know if EMDR is right for me?

EMDR is particularly effective for trauma, PTSD, anxiety, and disturbing memories. During our initial sessions, we'll assess whether EMDR would benefit your specific situation. Even if we don't proceed with EMDR, we'll use other evidence-based approaches tailored to your needs.
